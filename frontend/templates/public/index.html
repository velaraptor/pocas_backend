Bridging the gap between healthcare and social services!

MHP Portal integrates a city-wide database of social services, making it easy for healthcare providers to quickly find and refer patients to resources that can improve their health and well-being. From food and housing insecurity to transportation and employment challenges, MHP Portal helps identify and address the root causes of health issues, allowing for a more comprehensive approach to care.

Start making a positive impact on your patients' health and well-being today!

Ease of Use

Designed for easy use on any web-enabled device, the MHP Portal empowers healthcare providers with the tools they need to make a real difference in the lives of their patients. Say goodbye to endless search engines and outdated databases, and hello to a new way of delivering care that prioritizes the social and economic factors that impact health.

Mobile and Desktop Friendly

Whether you have a laptop, mobile phone, or tablet, MHP Portal will work for you!

Made for Healthcare Providers in Mind

We use the Accountable Health Communities Health-Related Social Needs Screening Tool which is a validated SDOH questionnaire through CMS (Center for Medicare and Medicaid Services).

Export Easily
